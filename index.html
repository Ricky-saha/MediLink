Do you Know About Us?
We introduce MedLink, a telemedicine kiosk integrated with a cutting-edge web application, to address healthcare difficulties in rural India. This advancement simplifies access to medical expertise by eliminating geographical barriers. The kiosk acts as a digital portal, converting stated complaints into actionable insights. MedLink is a symbol of progressive healthcare, ensuring rural communities receive the attention they deserve, combining technology and human touch

MedLink represents a paradigm shift in rural healthcare in India. It smoothly guides patients to the proper specialists by combining powerful AI and eSanjeevani. The novel "Abha Number" provides simple biometric logins and gives clinicians access to patient's medical records while maintaining a consistent medical history for each individual. We ensure timely medication supplies and attentive follow-ups in partnership with Asha workers. The kiosk approach expands on our goal by delivering unrivaled deployability. Every aspect of MedLink, from digital tools to grassroots collaboration, is intended to be a game changer, ushering in a new era of comprehensive and accessible healthcare in rural India.
